Vendor Standards for a Torah-friendly Simcha Platform

SimchaMap is not an open public listing board. Vendors are reviewed before being added, and listings must be appropriate for frum families planning simchos.

Frum Fit

Vendors must be comfortable serving frum families and respectful of the standards expected at Orthodox Jewish simchos.

  • No inappropriate public portfolio content
  • Respectful behavior and communication
  • Experience with simcha expectations preferred

Tznius & Photos

Photography, video, beauty, styling, and entertainment vendors must present themselves with modest, appropriate examples.

  • No immodest gallery images
  • No inappropriate promotional material
  • Listings may be removed if standards change

Kashrus & Events

Food, venue, and event-service listings must clearly fit the needs of kosher simchos and disclose relevant details.

  • Kosher standards must be clear
  • Hall and caterer details should be accurate
  • Families should confirm details before booking
Review rule: If a vendor does not match frum community standards, including photographers or videographers with immodest photos in their public work, they should not be listed on SimchaMap.
