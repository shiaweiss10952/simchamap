Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Overview

SimchaMap helps families plan simchas by offering planning tools, guides, and local vendor information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, account login information, planning checklist data, and messages or form submissions you send through the website.

Google Sign-In

If you sign in with Google, we use Google authentication through Supabase. We may receive basic account information such as your email address, name, and profile identifier. We use this information only to create and manage your SimchaMap account.

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To save your planning checklist and dashboard progress.
  • To improve SimchaMap features, content, and user experience.
  • To respond to questions, support requests, or vendor inquiries.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help operate the website, such as Supabase for authentication and database storage, only as needed to provide the service.

Data Storage and Security

Account and checklist data may be stored using Supabase. We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information, but no online service can guarantee perfect security.

Your Choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information. You may also stop using your account at any time.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at hello@simchamap.com.
