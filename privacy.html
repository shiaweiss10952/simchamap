Privacy Policy

SimchaMap helps users find vendors and organize simcha planning details. This page explains what information may be collected and how it is used.

Information we collect

When you create an account or use organizer tools, we may collect your email address, saved vendor notes, budget entries, calendar items, checklist details, and profile information you choose to provide.

How we use information

We use information to provide account access, save organizer details, improve the website, and help users manage simcha planning. We do not sell personal information.

Third-party services

SimchaMap may use services such as Supabase for authentication and data storage, and Google sign-in for account access. Those services may process information under their own privacy policies.

Data choices

You may choose not to add optional organizer information. You can request account information removal by contacting the site owner.

Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect user information. No online service can guarantee complete security.

Contact

For privacy questions or account removal requests, contact the SimchaMap site owner.
