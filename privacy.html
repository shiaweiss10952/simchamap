Privacy Policy

SimchaMap helps users discover event vendors and organize simcha planning details. This policy explains what information may be collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect

When you create an account or use organizer tools, we may collect basic account information such as your email address, profile details you provide, saved vendor notes, budget entries, calendar items, and planning checklist information.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide account access, save your organizer details, improve the website, and help you manage vendor planning. We do not sell personal information.

Third-Party Services

SimchaMap may use services such as Supabase for authentication and data storage, and Google sign-in for account access. Those services may process information according to their own privacy policies.

Data Choices

You may choose not to add optional organizer information. You can request removal of your account information by contacting the site owner.

Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect user information. No website or online service can guarantee complete security.

Contact

For privacy questions or account removal requests, contact the SimchaMap site owner.
