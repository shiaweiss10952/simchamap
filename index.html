Planning Guide

Your Simcha Timeline

A Bar Mitzvah takes about 12–18 months to plan. Here's when to book what.

18 Months Before

Set the Date & Book the Hall

Confirm the Bar Mitzvah date with your rabbi. Reserve your venue — the best halls fill up fast.

12 Months Before

Photographer, Band & Caterer

Book your photographer, videographer, band or DJ, and caterer. These vendors book out a year in advance.

9 Months Before

Invitations & Guest List

Finalize your guest list, design your invitations, and begin addressing. Order benchers and kippot.

6 Months Before

Décor, Theme & Clothing

Lock in your theme and décor. Start shopping for the Bar Mitzvah boy's suit and family outfits.

3 Months Before

Send Invitations

Mail invitations 6–8 weeks before the event. Book transportation and hotel room blocks for guests.

1 Month Before

Final Confirmations

Confirm headcount with caterer, finalize seating chart, confirm all vendors, and prepare payments.

The Big Day

Celebrate!

You've planned everything. Now enjoy every moment of this incredible milestone. Mazel Tov!
