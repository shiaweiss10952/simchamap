Timeline

What to do when.

12-18 months

Set the foundation

Choose a date, book the hall, confirm main family expectations, and begin the budget.

9-12 months

Book major vendors

Caterer, music, photographer after tznius review, decor, and any high-demand services.

6 months

Details and guests

Invitations, clothing, accommodations, transportation, and family task assignments.

3 months

Confirm and pay

Review contracts, deposits, payment due dates, and vendor communication notes.

Final month

Lock it in

Headcount, seating, timing, balances, and what must be brought to the hall.

Final week

Day-of packet

Phone numbers, checks/cash, schedule, family reminders, and emergency backup notes.

Organizer workflow

Put each decision somewhere.

Every vendor you call should go into Vendor CRM. Every price goes into Budget. Every appointment and payment due date goes into Calendar.

Vendor standards

Review visual vendors carefully.

Photographers, video, styling, beauty, clothing, and decor vendors should not be listed or booked from this site unless public photos and materials are appropriate.
